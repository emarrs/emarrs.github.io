No Poster Show promotional materials. Thinking of ways to generate interest and deliver imformation about an upcoming show to the public without using any posters. What's a better way to get someone to come to your show than by making them solve puzzles to find out about it? Puzzles are perfect: they make you feel smart which puts you in a good mood, and they are just plain fun. Each of these puzzles contains snippets of information about the date, time, and name of the No Poster Show. Coloring books were left on dining hall and common area tables (places where people tend to kill time) along with crayons, and then re-collected after a day.

Crayon on printed coloring books.
8.5"x11"
