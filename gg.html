Genuine and Ginger
Design Internship

I spent the summer of 2017 as an intern at an Albuquerque jewelry startup, Genuine & Ginger (now named Tumble). I designed stickers, newsletters, promotional graphics & emails, organized events, and generated content for publication and social media (mainly photography). I also designed a 30-page poetic lookbook containing photographs of the jewelry paired with my own imagery.
