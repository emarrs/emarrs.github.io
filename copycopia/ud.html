    playgiarism

    The practice of takingTAKING someone else's work or ideas and contributing to their authorship by researching, reworking, engaging, and playing with it, while taking measures to appropriately credit original work.

    "Hey bro, I saw a publication exactly like that at Printed Matter in Brooklyn. Wanna explain why you thought you'd get away with stealing it?"

    "Glad to! Actually, what i'm doing is playgiarizing--I'm very inspired by this publication and feel that rather than trying to come up with my own publication with a forcibly different framework (even though it'd be the same conceptually) just so I could say the idea was my own, I figured I could work directly off of the existing publication, contributing my own ideas, and strengthening it as a whole through collaboration. When I'm done, I'm going to send it to the author of the original, and see if they like it and want to work together!"

    Anti-Vaccuumization
