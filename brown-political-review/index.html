Brown Political Review. In the spring of 2019, my classmate Jae Won Kim and I took over as design directors for Brown Political Review, a quarterly magazine consisting of articles written by students at Brown University and illustrated by RISD students. Over the last four issues leading up to the publication's pandemic-induced extinction(?), Jae Won and I reinvented the editorial design system.
