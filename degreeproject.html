Post-Authentic Sincerity

Graphic Design Degree Project. I wrote an essay and made a website to contain it.
The writing analyzes realness, nature, and default systems in the context of graphic design, and proposes a new post-authentic approach to design which acknowledges its function as commodity service, rather than an inherently virtuous, innnocent or apolitical craft. Visit the site!

Written, designed and programmed in the Spring of 2020.
