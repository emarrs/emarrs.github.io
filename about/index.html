Welcome to Libby's [almost-purely] HTML and CSS website. About Libby: She enjoys print and web publications, complex information systems, creative and non-creative writing, drawing and arranging letterforms, writing HTML, and sewing clothes. Originally she is from New Mexico. You can send her an email at marrsec@gmail.com to inquire about recent work and freelance availability.
