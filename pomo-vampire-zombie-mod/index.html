Zombie Mods vs. Pomo Vampires. I typeset four essays written on the subject of Modernism vs. Postmodernism by notable designers with very different opinions. All of the essays flow simultaneously, creating discourse among each other. They seem to be arguing directly, taking personal jabs at each other, so I decided to supplement the greater narrative with characters: the Postmodernist Vampire and the Modernist Zombie, always at battle, neither winning in the end.

essays by Katherine McCoy, Massimo Vignelli,
Mr. Keedy & Paul Stiff
Original illustrations by me (Libby Marrs)
