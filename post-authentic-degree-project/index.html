Graphic Design Degree Project. I wrote an essay and made a website to contain it.
The writing explores the ideas of realness, nature, and default systems in the context of graphic design. It also proposes a new post-authentic approach to graphic design which acknowledges its function as commodity service, rather than an inherently virtuous, innnocent or apolitical craft. Visit the site!

Written, designed and programmed in the Spring of 2020.
