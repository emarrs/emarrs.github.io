Digitally Printed Wearables. Recent experiments desiging into apparel patterns, then digitally printing onto fabric and sewing tee-shirts. I'm interested in the intersection of language, graphic design, and apparel: how do we wear words? Thinking about novelty, virtue-signalling, and a cultural obsession with originality & specificity. Series to be continued? I don't know...hopefully.
